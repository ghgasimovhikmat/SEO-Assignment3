Delicious Web Development Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter, sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract, and mix until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt; gradually blend into the creamed mixture.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes or until lightly golden.
